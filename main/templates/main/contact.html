Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical response time for new inquiries?

We pride ourselves on rapid response times. Our solutions architects typically respond within 4 hours during business hours, and always within 24 hours. For urgent matters, please call our main line and request priority assistance.

What information should I include in my project inquiry?

To help us provide the most accurate proposal, please include: your business objectives, current challenges, timeline expectations, budget range, and any specific technical requirements. The more detail you provide, the better we can tailor our solution to your needs.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer complimentary initial consultations for all qualified enterprise inquiries. During this 60-minute session, our solutions architect will analyze your requirements and provide high-level recommendations. There's no obligation to proceed further.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have deep expertise in finance & banking, healthcare, technology, retail & e-commerce, and manufacturing. However, we work with enterprises across all sectors. Our methodology is adaptable to any industry with complex digital transformation needs.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple implementations can take 4-8 weeks, while enterprise-scale digital transformations typically range from 6-18 months. After our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed project roadmap with specific milestones.
