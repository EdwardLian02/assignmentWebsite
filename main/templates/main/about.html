The Beginning

Born from a Simple Idea

AI-Solution was founded in 2018 by a group of engineers who were tired of seeing enterprise software that was powerful but painful to use. We believed there had to be a better way—software that combined enterprise-grade robustness with consumer-grade elegance.

Starting with just three clients in the fintech space, we proved that our approach worked. By focusing on intuitive user experiences alongside scalable architecture, we delivered solutions that employees actually enjoyed using.
